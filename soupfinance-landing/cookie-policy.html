Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Tech At Scale Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at www.soupfinance.com or use the SoupFinance application ("Service"). This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is being used.

Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until deleted or until they expire) or "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the Service to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies to keep you logged in
  • Session management cookies
  • Security cookies to prevent fraud and protect your account
  • Load balancing cookies to ensure the Service performs well

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Remembering your preferences (language, currency, timezone)
  • Remembering your display settings
  • Storing information you've entered to prevent re-entry

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our Service:

  • Counting visitors and page views
  • Understanding how users navigate through the Service
  • Identifying which features are most used
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our communications

2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we may use cookies to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements on other websites
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Limit the number of times you see an advertisement

5. Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies:

6. Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

6.2 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Allow cookies from specific websites only

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6.3 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

6.4 Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable essential cookies, some features of the Service may not work properly, including:

  • You may not be able to log in or stay logged in
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some security features may not function

7. Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no uniform standard for responding to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT signals, but we honor the cookie preferences you set through our consent mechanism.
